This Tableau project explores how online customer reviews influence consumer trust, purchasing decisions, and potential sales outcomes. Using a dataset of Amazon product reviews, the analysis focuses on review sentiment, rating distribution, trustworthiness indicators, and recurring complaint themes.

The purpose of this dashboard is to transform raw review data into clear, actionable insights that businesses can use to improve product performance and customer satisfaction.

1. Star Ratings Distribution

2. Sentiment Breakdown (Positive / Neutral / Negative)

3. Helpfulness by Rating

Helpfulness percentage was calculated to measure which reviews are considered most influential and trustworthy by other consumers.

Instead of an unclear word cloud, the dashboard identifies the most frequent negative review summaries, revealing the strongest drivers of dissatisfaction.

5. Average Rating Trend Over Time

Tracks how customer satisfaction changes over time, offering insight into consistency and long-term brand performance.

This step-by-step storytelling approach ensures the dashboard communicates not only data, but meaning and business impact.

‚úÖ Key Insights & Business Recommendations

Positive reviews dominate, but recurring negative themes still strongly shape consumer trust.

Reviews with extreme ratings tend to receive the highest helpfulness votes, meaning they influence buyers most.

Common complaint themes (quality issues, dissatisfaction, refund concerns) represent clear improvement opportunities.

To improve sales outcomes, sellers should:

Strengthen quality control

Respond proactively to negative feedback

Encourage detailed and helpful customer reviews

Use sentiment monitoring as an early warning system
